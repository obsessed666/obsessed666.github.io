Ronin-SC Comprehensive Camera & Lens Compatibility Testing - Continuously Updated
With the release of Ronin-SC, many avid photographers will undoubtedly be wondering about the compatibility of their current equipment (i.e., cameras and lenses). To thoroughly explore the compatibility of Ronin-SC, DJI has undergone a comprehensive installation test that covers most mainstream cameras and lenses available on the market. At present, over 70 cameras and lenses have been tested. Through these installation tests, DJI hopes to help users be more informed on whether or not Ronin-SC is their best choice.
Note: The following cameras and lenses were tested by DJI, but do not represent all of the devices that are compatible with Ronin-SC. For cameras or lenses that are not included on the list, please refer to actual use experience.
